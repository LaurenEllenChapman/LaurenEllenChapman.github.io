Indiana is one of the few Midwest holdouts on any form of cannabis legalization. A small protest gathered on the steps of the Indiana Statehouse in January 2020 and passed around cannabis products — including Bill Levin, the Grand Poobah of the Indiana Church of Cannabis. (Lauren Chapman/IPB News)

Indianapolis was among the U.S. cities who hosted Black Lives Matter protests the last weekend of May 2020. The Indianapolis Metropolitan Police Department shot and killed Dreajson Reed the previous summer, along with four others in a 24-hour period. (Lauren Chapman/IPB News)
